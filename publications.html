Intersectionality in Convict Criminology

Co-authored with: J. Renee Trombley

Description: This book expands the field of convict criminology to include the voices of women, people of color, and LGBTQ+ individuals. It centers lived experience as valid scholarship and shifts narratives about who gets to produce knowledge in criminal justice studies.

Audience: Scholars, students, activists, and anyone seeking more inclusive criminal justice frameworks.
